Lee, Mildred Anita   1915 - 2004 Funeral services for Mildred Anita Lee, 89, of Hampton, Ill., are 10 a.m. Friday at Van Hoe Funeral Home Ltd., East Moline. Burial is in Hampton Cemetery. Visitation is 4-7 p.m. today at the funeral home. Mrs. Lee died Wednesday, Aug. 4, 2004, at Rosewood Care Center, Moline. Memorials may be made to Hampton United Methodist Church, where she was a member.
Mildred Ferman was born May 15, 1915, in East Moline, the daughter of Grover and Virgie (Rose) Ferman. She married Joseph Lee, Aug. 11, 1934, in East Moline. He died March 26, 1984.
Survivors include son and daughter-in-law, Robert and Carol Lee, Silvis, Ill.; grandchildren, Kimberly Gibbs, Mark (Judy) Johnson, Kay (Dr. Curt) Rexroth, Rhonda (Craig) Brooks, Susan (Chris) Mekus, and Brian (Lisa) Lee; and 12 great-grandchildren.
Daughters, JoAnn Lee Cooper and Joyce Lee Johnson, and seven brothers and sisters preceded her in death.
