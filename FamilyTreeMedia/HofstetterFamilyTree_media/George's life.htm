George Washington was the youngest child of James & Hannah. He was an "Uncle" 5 times before he was born. He moved his wife and children to Grundy Co., Mo. on 1870. In 1883 he went onto Kansas and settled in Burlingame, Osage Co. Kansas. And opened a boarding house for railroad men called the "Ohio House". It burned in 1897. Hewas also remembered as a weaver of carpets. George was a quiet soft-spoken man with a good disposition.
