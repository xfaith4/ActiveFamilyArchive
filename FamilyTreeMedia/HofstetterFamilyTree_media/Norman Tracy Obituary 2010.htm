Norman Clarence Tracy, 81, of Aledo, went to be with his Lord and Savior on Thursday, April 29, 2010, at Galesburg Cottage Hospital, with his family at his side.

Funeral services were held at 2 p.m. on Monday at Trinity Presbyterian Church, Aledo. Burial was at Aledo Cemetery. Visitation was from 2 to 6 p.m. Sunday at Trinity Presbyterian Church. Memorials may be made to the Trinity Presbyterian Church or the AMT Medic, Aledo. Speer Funeral Home, Aledo, is assisting the family.

Norman was born on Dec. 24, 1928 in Fair Grove, Missouri to Clarence and Nola (Blair) Tracy. He married Cherie Brown on Sept. 25, 1955. They farmed in Ohio Grove Township until retiring in 2001. They moved to Aledo in 2006.

Norman was a member of Mercer County Farm Bureau and the ASC Board of Directors. He was presented the conservation Farm Family Award in 1982 by AISWCD & WGN Broadcasting. He was an avid bowler and participated in Burgess softball in his earlier years. Norman loved to travel, to tell stories and to fly. He loved trains and enjoyed nature. His true joy was his family and spending time with them at the lake. He also enjoyed repairing things, and could work on anything, but specialized in clocks.
